# 🌾 Knowledge Farm

**Knowledge Farm** is a cozy, educational farming simulation game inspired by the aesthetic of *Animal Crossing*. It serves as an interactive introduction to **Classic Chinese Agronomy**, where players learn the science behind essential crops while building their farm empire through knowledge.

## 🌏 Project Vision: The Classic Crop Series
This project is an ongoing exploration of China's rich agricultural heritage. While the current version features a deep dive into **Rice Cultivation**, the game is designed to be an expandable platform for various classic crops.

- **Current Chapter**: 🌾 **The Rice Harvest** (Comprehensive trivia on varieties, geography, and processing).
- **In Development**: 🍑 **The Orchard Update** (Featuring Peaches and other classic fruits).
- **Future Goals**: Expanding to include traditional medicinal herbs, tea, and diverse vegetables.

## 🎮 Key Features

- **Agronomy-Driven Growth**: Unlike typical farming sims, your crops grow based on your knowledge. Answer trivia questions grounded in real agricultural science to progress through growth stages.
- **Dynamic Combo System**: Master the material to trigger "Combo" streaks! Features a custom-designed UI with bouncy animations and win-streak badges.
- **Doctor Mi’s Random Subsidy**: A rogue-like surprise mechanic. Every harvest has a 30% chance to trigger a random cash bonus (1-100 Bud-Coins) from **Doctor Mi 👩‍🔬**, adding excitement to the final phase.
- **Honor Achievement Cards**: Automatically generates a beautiful summary card after each harvest. Display your "Farming Title," accuracy, and total earnings—perfect for social sharing.
- **Dynamic Rescue Missions**: Protect your crops from real-world farming challenges like floods, droughts, and pests through time-sensitive mini-games with a high-stakes countdown UI.
- **Bud-Coin (芽币) Economy**: A fully integrated game economy. Start with a free trial, then earn and reinvest "Bud-Coins" to sustain your farm. Crop quality (and price) depends on your trivia accuracy.

## 🛠️ How to Play

1. **Visit the Field**: Start at your "Plot Under Development" to see the potential of your farm.
2. **Shop for Seeds**: Visit the Nook-inspired Seed Shop to purchase your next challenge. (First order is free!)
3. **Learn & Grow**: Progress through stages (*Seeding*, *Growing*, *Mature*) by solving trivia. Your accuracy determines if you harvest **Special Grade**, **Standard**, or **Broken Rice**.
4. **Harvest & Share**: Sell your crops for Bud-Coins, unlock new entries in your Handbook, and screenshot your Achievement Card to share your success.

## 🚀 Deployment & Tech
Built with **Vanilla HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript**. No heavy frameworks—just clean, fast, and responsive code.

- **Frontend**: CSS Grid & Flexbox for the mobile-first "Phone Container" UI.
- **Logic**: Custom state management for growth stages and randomized event triggers.
- **Animations**: CSS Keyframes for the "breathe" and "combo" effects.
